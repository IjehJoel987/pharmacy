How to Use Our Website

1

Browse Products

Visit our Products page to see our extensive catalog of medications. Use the search bar to quickly find specific items you need.

2

Add to Cart

Click "Add to Cart" for any medications you want to order. You can adjust quantities in your cart later.

3

Review Your Cart

Click the cart icon to review your selected items. You can modify quantities or remove items as needed.

4

Checkout via WhatsApp

When ready, click "Checkout via WhatsApp". This will open WhatsApp with your order details pre-filled.

5

Complete Your Order

Our pharmacy staff will provide pricing, confirm availability, and arrange payment and delivery through WhatsApp.

Important Notes:

  • Prices are not displayed online as they are subject to change
  • Final pricing and availability will be confirmed via WhatsApp
  • Prescription medications require valid prescriptions
  • If you can't find a specific medication, use our "Contact Us" feature
